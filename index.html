The Game

Meteor Swarm is a retro-inspired action game combining simple gameplay, fast-paced action, and the thrill of getting the highest score on the high-score table. Blast your way through the never-ending swarm of Meteors in any of the 3 exciting game modes!

Features

  • 3 Exciting Game Modes!
  • Fast-paced gameplay!
  • Local High-Scores!
